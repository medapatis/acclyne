Pareto Principle


We love automation and automated processes. Keeping the 80:20 rough rule in mind can save you money and time, and improve your chance of success by keeping you focused on what’s important. Different areas where Pareto Principle can be used; In development, 80% of the task is completed by 20% coding. In user experience, 80% of use cases can be completed by 20% of interface. In Quality Assurance, knowing that 80% of the people only use 20% of the application features, you should be focusing on the 20% that matters.
